About Junlin Group

Junlin Group is a diversified company consisting of the following divisions:

  • Junlin Solutions
  • Junlin Global Sourcing
  • Junlin Wealth

Junlin Solutions

Junlin Solutions offers advisory and consultation services, global trading, and online and offline retail solutions. We provide:

  • Expert advisory and consultation services
  • Global trading opportunities
  • Online and offline retail solutions

Junlin Global Sourcing specializes in global sourcing for brands interested in entering Far East Markets, as well as brands looking for manufacturing from the Far East to Global Markets. Our services include:

  • Global sourcing for brands targeting Far East markets
  • Manufacturing solutions for brands looking to expand globally
  • Quality control services in the Far East

Junlin Wealth

Junlin Wealth assists firms in achieving carbon neutrality and offers investment opportunities in the New Zealand forestry market. Our services include:

  • Assistance in achieving carbon neutrality
  • Investment options in the New Zealand forestry market
