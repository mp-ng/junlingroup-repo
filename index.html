About Junlin Group

Junlin Group is a diversified company consisting of the following divisions:

Junlin Solutions

Junlin Solutions offers advisory and consultation services, global trading, and online and offline retail solutions.

Junlin Global Sourcing

Junlin Global Sourcing specializes in global sourcing for brands interested in entering Far East Markets, as well as brands looking for manufacturing from the Far East to Global Markets.

Junlin Wealth

Junlin Wealth assists firms in achieving carbon neutrality and offers investment opportunities in the New Zealand forestry market.
